What is The Magic 8 Ball?

The magic 8 Ball originally is a famous 80's toy that can 'predict' the future by answering yes or no questions.

Step 1:

Type your (yes or no) Question in the white box below the 8 Ball.

Step 2:

Ckick the black arrow and see what the magic 8-Ball answers you!

Step 3:

Click the reload arrow at the bottom to clear and ask your next question.
